What is EcoFan?

Eco fan is the a next generation device that help reduce the use of electricity used by the computer cooling system.

Why EcoFan?

Many digital servers in the world are constantly using the electronic fan to cool down the system. This is a problem because those fan running non-stop, even at night. Eco Fan helps solve this problem by turn off the fan at night to save the energy usage and let the nature do its job while the sun is down.
